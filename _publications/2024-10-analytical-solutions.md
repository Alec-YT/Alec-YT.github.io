excerpt: 'Newwww analytical solutions are derived considering both face advance and time-dependent effects for deep cirular tunnels assuming a fractional viscoelastic-plastic constitutive behavior. We show that the new closed-form solutions can then easily be used for back-analysis of convergence data in order to determine the constitutive parameters of the rock. The methodology is applied to two different case studies: The Saint-Martin-la-Porte access gallery and the Fréjus road tunnel.'
